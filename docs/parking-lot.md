# Parking Lot

These ideas are intentionally outside the main ACT roadmap until the project
proves the next core milestone: one real backend action blocked on one real
phone clearance.

## Fleet And Platform Expansion

- Cross-backend fleet dashboards.
- Large-scale mission management.
- Fleet-level incident response.
- Cross-agent orchestration.
- Search across many backends, agents, missions, clearances, notices, and audit
  records.
- Long-running task supervision analytics.

## Multi-User And Enterprise

- User identities beyond the implicit owner.
- Role-based operator permissions.
- Multi-device conflict and delegation handling.
- Organization-level ownership.
- Audit export and retention administration.
- Policy review workflows for teams.

## Hosted And Marketplace Work

- Hosted relay or coordination service.
- Marketplace adapter packaging.
- SaaS business model.
- Managed push infrastructure.

## Advanced Policy Authoring

- Safety policy simulation.
- Policy proposal review queues.
- Policy templates by backend type.
- Organization-level policy promotion.

These may become valuable later, but they should not distract from the current
ACT proof point: backend-neutral clearances and handoffs with Hermes as adapter
#1.
